About Me

Hi there! I'm a current graduate student at Mills College and am set to graduate with my MA in Interdisciplinary Computer Science in May of 2017. I have a BA in English from Scripps College, where I became passionate about digital humanities and intersections of technology and language. I'm also a published poet and fiction writer and, in my free time, I've trained in competitive ballroom dancing and circus arts.

I'm often open to part-time software development or curriculum development jobs - if you're interested, feel free to contact me. In the meantime, please take a look at my site and explore some of my work!
